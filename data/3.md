# Corporate Travel and Expense Reimbursement Policy

All employee travel, client dinners, and hardware purchases must follow these financial guidelines to be eligible for reimbursement.

## Travel Budgets and Booking Rules
All corporate flights must be booked through Navan (formerly TripActions). Flights under 6 hours must be booked in Economy class. Flights exceeding 6 hours are eligible for Premium Economy. Business class requires written approval from your department VP. The maximum daily hotel budget is $250 for Tier 1 cities (NY, SF, London) and $150 for all other locations.

## Meals and Client Entertainment
The daily allowance for meals during travel is $75 (breakfast: $15, lunch: $20, dinner: $40). Client entertainment dinners must not exceed $120 per person, including gratuity. Alcohol purchases are capped at two drinks per person during business dinners.

## Receipt Submission and Deadlines
All expense reports must be submitted in Expensify within 30 days of the transaction. Receipts are mandatory for all transactions over $25. Any expense submitted without a valid receipt will be automatically rejected by the audit bot.
