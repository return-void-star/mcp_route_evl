# Legal Department: Non-Disclosure Agreement Guidelines

This document details our legal requirements regarding external contractors, intellectual property, and proprietary information.

## Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs)
Before sharing any confidential product mockups, codebase access, or financial metrics with external partners or vendors, a Mutual NDA must be signed. We use the standard template located in the Confluence Legal Space (`Legal_MNDA_v3.docx`). Any edits to this template made by the counterparty must be reviewed by legal-counsel@company.com.

## Intellectual Property (IP)
All code, designs, strategies, and documents created by employees during their employment are the sole property of the corporation (Work For Hire). Employees must not use company assets (laptops, servers, codebases) for personal side projects to prevent IP contamination.

## External Contractor Onboarding
Contractors must sign the Independent Contractor Agreement (ICA) before being granted corporate Okta access. Contractor accounts are automatically deactivated after 180 days unless a extension request is filed by their hiring manager.
