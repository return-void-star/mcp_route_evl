# Executive Committee: Q3 Strategic Alignment and Hiring Policy

This document details the executive decisions regarding market growth, expense management, and headcount planning.

## Financial Target Reductions
Due to macroeconomic changes, we are adjusting our Q3 revenue growth target from 24% YoY to 18% YoY. Departments are requested to reduce operational budgets by 10%. Non-essential travel (internal team offsites, conferences) is suspended until Q4.

## Headcount Planning and Hiring Freeze
We are instituting a temporary hiring freeze on all non-engineering roles for the remainder of Q3. Any exceptions to this freeze require direct sign-off from the COO and CEO. Engineering roles currently open in Salesforce, Product, and Security may proceed as planned, but starting dates must be batched on the 1st of each month.

## Focus Areas for R&D
Resources will be shifted from speculative projects to the integration of generative AI into our core product line. The timeline for the beta release of this AI feature is set for October 15th.
