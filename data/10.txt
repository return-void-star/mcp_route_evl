ENVIRONMENTAL HEALTH & SAFETY: EMERGENCY RESPONSE MANUAL

In the event of an emergency, remain calm and follow these procedures.

1. Fire Evacuation Protocol
If you hear the fire alarm:
- Immediately stop working and do not try to gather personal belongings.
- Walk (do not run) to the nearest emergency exit.
- Do not use the elevators under any circumstances.
- Assemble at the designated parking lot area (Zone A, marked by the yellow flag).
- The designated fire warden for the 3rd floor is Sarah Connor (Ext. 204).

2. Medical Emergencies
For minor injuries, first-aid kits are located in the kitchen cabinets on every floor. For serious medical emergencies, immediately dial 911 (or local emergency services) and notify the front desk security team at Ext. 100 so they can guide the paramedics to the correct elevator bank.

3. Severe Weather
In the event of a tornado warning, move away from external windows. Evacuate to the designated storm shelters located in the basement parking garage.
