FACILITIES MANAGEMENT: OFFICE RULES & VISITOR POLICIES

Welcome to our headquarters. Please respect the shared spaces and follow these office rules.

1. Meeting Room Bookings
Meeting rooms must be booked via Google Calendar. If a room is unoccupied 10 minutes after the start of a reservation, it is considered open for anyone to use. The video conferencing system password for the main boardroom is `BoardroomConference2026`.

2. Printing and Scanning
Printers are located on floors 2, 3, and 5. To release a print job, tap your employee badge on the scanner or type in the corporate print code: `9982`. Empty toner cartridges should be placed in the recycling bin next to the mailroom.

3. Visitor Access and Parking
All visitors must sign in at the reception desk on the first floor and wear a guest badge at all times. Employees must escort their guests. Validation for the basement parking garage is available at the front desk for clients.
