HUMAN RESOURCES: BENEFIT PLAN SUMMARY & POLICIES

This guide details the current corporate benefits, wellness stipends, and leave policies for all full-time salary employees.

1. Paid Time Off (PTO) and Vacation Accrual
All standard employees accrue 22 days of paid vacation per calendar year, accrued at a rate of 1.83 days per month. Unused vacation up to 5 days can carry over to the next calendar year, but must be used by March 31st or it will be forfeited. PTO requests must be submitted through the Workday portal at least 10 business days prior to the start of the leave.

2. Health and Wellness Stipends
The company reimburses up to $75 per month for physical wellness expenses. This covers gym memberships, yoga classes, or fitness trackers. To claim, submit your monthly invoice to expense-benefits@company.com with the subject line "Wellness Stipend [Month] [Name]".

3. Parental and Medical Leave
New biological or adoptive parents are eligible for 12 weeks of fully paid parental leave. This leave must be taken within the first year of the child's birth or placement. Medical leave requests exceeding 3 consecutive days require a signed doctor's note submitted to the HR benefits coordinator.
