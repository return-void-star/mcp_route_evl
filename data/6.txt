SALES DIVISION: ENTERPRISE PLAYBOOK & DISCOUNTING RULES

This internal playbook outlines the sales pipeline steps, pricing rules, and approval boundaries for software sales.

1. Dealing with Enterprise Pricing
Our standard list price for the Enterprise SaaS tier is $45 per user per month, billed annually. The Minimum contract size for Enterprise tier is 50 seats ($27,000 ARR). 

2. Discounting Limits and Approval Levels
Account Executives (AEs) are authorized to offer discounts based on contract length and seat volume:
- Up to 10% discount: AE discretion (no approval needed).
- 11% to 20% discount: Requires written approval from the Regional Sales Director.
- 21% to 35% discount: Requires approval from the VP of Global Sales.
- Anything above 35%: Requires sign-off from the CFO.

3. Client Onboarding Hand-off
Once a contract is signed in DocuSign, change the Salesforce opportunity stage to "Closed Won". This triggers an automated Slack notification to #customer-success, who will schedule the kickoff call within 48 hours.
